turbomoleOutputProcessing.turbomoleOutputProcessing.calculate_localization_mo(c, s_mat, left, right)[source]¶

Calculates localization of mos 1 = c_l*s_lr*c_r + c_r*s_lr*c_l+c_l*s_ll*c_l + c_r*s_rr*c_r. The last two summands describe the localization in left and richt part (defined as Q_l and Q_r). The algorithm is not restricted to the left and right parts, but can be used to analyse the given mos. Thus the L-C, C-R coupling and localization can be analysed, too. :param param1: eigencectors :type param1: matrix :param param2: s matrix :type param2: matrix :param param3: left (matrix indices of left part start, matrix indices of left part end) :type param3: (int, int) :param param4: right (matrix indices of right part start, matrix indices of right part end) :type param4: (int, int)

Returns

List of tuples (Q_l, Q_r) (Localization of each mo)
